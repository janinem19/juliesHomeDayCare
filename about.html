About Julie’s Home Day Care

I take a ‘whole child approach’ to day care! This means your little ones will be valued for their individual personalities and will be supported and nurtured throughout their time with me!

We understand that today’s families are very busy and that you are looking for the best care possible to nurture your little ones and support their early learning journey!

As a professional childcare educator and a mum myself, I understand how important it is four our children to feel safe and secure and aim to create an environment that feels like a second home. I have a knowledge of education principles and am qualified in this area, holding a Diploma of Children’s Services.

Please get in touch with me to have a chat and to come and tour our space – I am looking forward to meeting your family!
